How It Works

Simple. Fast. Transparent.

1

Post Your Request

Describe what you need and your location. Our AI Scope Builder helps you create a detailed job description.

2

Get Matched

Our smart system matches your request to verified providers in your area within minutes.

3

Compare Bids

Service providers compete to offer you the best deals. Review price, timeframe, and ratings.

4

Accept & Connect

Accept the bid that suits you, a contract is auto-generated, and you can message your provider directly.

5

Pay Securely

Secure payments via Stripe with automatic receipts, commission handling, and provider payouts.

Launch Features

Everything You Need at Launch

⇅

Reverse Bidding System

Consumers create jobs, providers submit competitive bids. Provider identity stays hidden until acceptance. Price, timeframe, and custom filters included.

⚙

AI Scope Builder

Chat-based flow that asks smart questions and generates a complete job scope automatically.

💳

Secure Payments (Stripe)

Provider payouts, commission logic, consumer service fees, and full transaction receipts — all built in.

📝

Auto Contract Generator

Standardized contracts generated automatically once a bid is accepted. No paperwork needed.

✅

Provider Verification

License and insurance uploads with admin approval. Automatic verification where possible for trust and safety.

💬

In-App Messaging

Internal messaging system activates after bid acceptance. Share photos, voice notes, and details securely.

📍

Geolocation & Radius Filtering

Find providers near you. Filter by service type, radius, availability, and custom preferences.

⭐

Ratings System

Internal Service Savior ratings collected from day one. External ratings import planned for Phase 2.

🛠

Dual Dashboards

Dedicated dashboards for both providers and consumers — manage jobs, bids, messages, availability, and notifications.

🚀

Marketing Automation

Scheduled outreach bot for onboarding new service providers and growing the network.

🔐

Full Admin Panel

Manage users, approve providers, view disputes, manage service categories, and access the financial dashboard.

📱

Website + Mobile App

Single app with two login types — consumer and service pro — each with their own tailored experience.
